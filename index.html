What's Special About BitwiseHacks?

BitwiseHacks is a unique opportunity for high school and university students to participate in a fast-paced hackathon environment, while competing against their peers for unique opportunities in Hong Kong's vibrant tech and startup space. Being open exclusively to students, this is the perfect time to show off your skills, or even just try out coding, in a period of 24 hours!

At Bitwise, Expect to:

Meet Others

You'll have a lot of time to meet, and network with, like-minded techies and entrepeneurs. Often overlooked, this can be one of the most important hackathon offerings.

Code

Coding is a big part of the hackathon experience, and you'll have 24 hours to program your project, alone, or better yet, with a team.

Compete

After you have been hard at work for 24 hours, there will be a final round of demos which will be judged by leading tech professionals in Hong Kong.

Find Opportunities

You will be competing for chances at unique opportunities and internships from our sponsors. We will be releasing more of these details soon.
